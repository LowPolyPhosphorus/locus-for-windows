No more half-measures. Locus goes further than other focus apps — it's a system, not just a timer.

Smart App Blocking

AI blocks all apps and websites unless they're on your allowlist or obviously related to your task.

URL Filtering

Domain-level blocking across Chrome, Safari, and other browsers. The web stays locked until you're done.

Connectors

Sign in with Notion or any calendar app and your sessions auto-populate from your planner.

AI-Evaluated Unblocks

Need to access a blocked site? Write your reason and an LLM decides if it's actually on-task.

Smart Re-Blocking

Approved sites get blocked again if you drift off-task. No more lying or loopholes.

Customization

Huge amounts of customization make it your own. Completely open-source for deeper changes.

AI-Evaluated Unblocks
Your excuses get graded.

When you try to access a blocked site, you have to explain why. AI reads your reason and decides — based on what you said you're working on — whether to let you through.
